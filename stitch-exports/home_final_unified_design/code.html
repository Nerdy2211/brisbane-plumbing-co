Technical FAQ

What are your licensing credentials? expand_more
We are fully licensed by the QBCC (Queensland Building and Construction Commission) and carry comprehensive public liability insurance. Every member of our team is a qualified trade professional.
Do you offer emergency response? expand_more
We focus on scheduled maintenance and planned projects to ensure we can provide the highest quality service to our regular clients. While we prioritize urgent issues for existing clients during business hours, we are not a dedicated 24/7 emergency service.
How do you charge for your services? expand_more
We provide upfront, transparent quotes based on the scope of work. We do not use "low-ball" hourly rates with hidden fees. You will know exactly what the investment is before we begin.
Which areas of Brisbane do you cover? expand_more
Our core service area includes Brisbane CBD and all surrounding suburbs within a 30km radius, extending from North Lakes to Logan and out to Ipswich.
